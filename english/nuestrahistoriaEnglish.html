Our history

ALQUIMIA INTERNACIONAL was born in 2013 from the vision of its three founders, each of whom brings together More than 20 years of experience in the management of multinational international transport companies cargo, in the direction and supervision of projects of different kinds, and in the commercialization of products, in countries of Europe, North America, Central America and the Caribbean. It is here in the Dominican Republic, a land that welcomed them more than 10 years ago, where their motivation for resolve the shortcomings of the local market in terms of unnecessary cost overruns, short transit times unreliable, lack of communication and personalized attention, From its beginnings until today the company has been very successful thanks to the strategic collaboration both with clients and suppliers, as well as with other cargo agents without representation or license in the Republic Dominican. The fusion of identities of its founders, together with their extensive experience in the sector, is what makes ALQUIMIA INTERNACIONAL is the perfect ally in the Dominican Republic for clients and freight forwarders.
